Privacy & How Sealed Letter Works

Sealed Letter lets you write a private note, lock it with a passkey, and retrieve it on any device—instantly, no account required.

How your note is protected

  • Your note is encrypted in your browser using modern AES‑256 encryption.
  • The only key is your passkey; we never store it.
  • The encrypted note is stored on Firebase, but it cannot be read without your passkey.
  • We do not collect or store personal information or logs tied to your data.

What you should know

  • There is no account, no email, no password recovery.
  • If you forget your passkey, your note cannot be recovered.
  • Always keep your passkey safe and never share it with others.

For more details on encryption, see the source code on GitHub.
